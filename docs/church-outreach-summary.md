# Church Outreach Summary

## What is Find Your Church Palacios?

Find Your Church Palacios is a ministry project powered by El Roi Digital Ministries. It helps residents, visitors, and families find local churches, view service times, and connect with church communities in the Palacios area.

Palacios is the first local launch in a larger long-term vision to help more communities make local church information easy to find, accurate, and accessible.

## Who created it?

Find Your Church Palacios was created by El Roi Digital Ministries as a ministry-focused local church directory project.

## How churches get listed

Churches can:

- submit a new listing through the public website
- be added by the ministry team
- claim an existing listing and request representative access

Listings are reviewed before they appear publicly.

## How churches claim a listing

If a church already has a public listing:

1. Visit the church profile.
2. Click `Claim This Church`.
3. Sign in or create an account.
4. Submit the request with your role and explanation.
5. The request is reviewed by the ministry team.

## How updates work

Once a church representative is approved:

- the church can sign in to the representative portal
- updates can either publish immediately or go through admin review, depending on the church setting
- the church can message the review team
- the primary owner can invite one editor

## Donation-supported ministry

Find Your Church Palacios is offered as a donation-supported ministry project. We do not want cost to keep a church from being searchable, visible, and easy to connect with.

If a church is able to donate, that support helps keep the directory free and helps this ministry grow. If a church cannot donate, it can still be listed.

## Short FAQ

### Is there a cost to be listed?

No. Listing is not locked behind payment.

### Can we update our church information?

Yes. A church can submit a listing update or claim its listing and use the representative portal once approved.

### Who can claim a church listing?

Pastors, staff members, or other authorized representatives of the church.

### How long does approval take?

The goal is usually within 24 hours.

### Can we donate even though listing is free?

Yes. Donations are welcome, but never required.

### What if our church cannot donate?

Your church can still be listed. Donations help support the ministry, but they are never required for inclusion.

### What if our information is wrong?

Contact the ministry team or submit an update request so the listing can be corrected.

### Can a church be removed?

Yes. Listings may be edited, denied, or removed if information is inaccurate, misleading, inappropriate, or no longer belongs in the directory.

### How does a church contact us?

Churches can contact the ministry team at `support@findyourchurchpalacios.org`.
